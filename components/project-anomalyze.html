Anomalyze: AI-Powered Cybersecurity

Generative AI Hackathon at Anhad’25, IIT Jammu

The Challenge

Our project aimed to ease the day-to-day responsibilities of a SOC (Security Operations Center) Analyst...

Our Solution

We built a system that could analyze massive volumes of IP traffic, detect anomalies, and flag suspicious IPs in real-time...

Here's How We Built It:

  • Data Collection & Preprocessing: We started by collecting 220 GB of IP log data...
  • Vectorization: The raw logs were transformed into structured numerical vectors...
  • Model Training: We then trained a Machine Learning model capable of identifying suspicious patterns...
  • Real-Time Threat Detection: Our final system can now scan incoming logs on the fly...

Outcome & Reflection

What made this project special wasn’t just the code... Excited to keep building, learning, and collaborating.
